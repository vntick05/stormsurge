## Executive Summary
This RFP package outlines the requirements for a project that involves managing and delivering IT services. The project aims to close projects that meet or exceed customer objectives, create clear and attainable project objectives, and manage constraints of cost, time, scope, and quality. The Offeror will be responsible for providing technical assistance and support related to computer systems, hardware, or software, and for ensuring proper relationships are established between customers, teaming partners, and vendors.

## Scope Of Work
The Scope of Work includes:

* Managing and delivering IT services
* Closing projects that meet or exceed customer objectives
* Creating clear and attainable project objectives
* Managing constraints of cost, time, scope, and quality
* Providing technical assistance and support related to computer systems, hardware, or software
* Ensuring proper relationships are established between customers, teaming partners, and vendors
